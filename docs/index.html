PID Controller Car Simulator


Instructions: You can choose drive the car or do a free hands drive (Automatic).

Objetive: Full Brake in Position z=100 (Center of the green square) as soon as possible.
You have three seconds to set up the configuration in mode Simulation.


Button Configuration:

Action Key
Accelerate w
Brake Space key
Reverse s
Turn Left a
Turn Right d
Chase Camera Number 1 key
Chase Camera Side Number 2 key
